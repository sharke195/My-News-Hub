Major Running News in Cheboygan

The cross country team in Cheboygan has been making some serious moves against their competition recently. Their most recent win was in Indian River on Monday the 23rd. The girls team took first out of ten other teams! This is one of many wins for the girls team this season, the team may be young but it has grown a lot in the past couple seasons. In the 21-22 season the team only had seven people including boys and girls, now the team has grown to be around nineteen runners. The girls team has more people with eleven, the boys have just over enough for a varsity team with eight runners.


Captains

The Cheboygan cross country team is lead by senior capatins Sammy Harke and Evan Blaskowski. Harke has been running since her 6th grade season making this her seventh year running. She has been capatin of the highschool team for the past three years. Blaskowski joined his sophmore year which makes this his third year running. He has been the captain of the highschool team for two years.


When asked about her sport Harke said I love the team more than the actual sport, they make the sport. Blaskowski was not around for a quote at the time. Both runners are set to graduate this year. Blaskowski will go on to powerlift in the winter and play baseball in the spring. Harke will also go to powerlifting in the winter but she is heading to track in the spring.
